    • Canteen Ordering Management System

    A corporate client requested to improve their Staff Lunch-time experience by introducing an improved food/canteen ordering system. As part of Business Analysis and Improvement, we initiated information requirement gathering from the identified stakeholders, created a RACI matrix, developed a Process Map (AS-IS and TO-BE) as well User Stories and Criteria & Acceptance Testing. These information were loaded on Jira and the developers were tasked to develop the appropriate Ordering Application and their performance were monitored through regular sprints sessions.
